Provide instructions on how course staff can create a login for them to use to test your system:

1: On the welcome page, click Login/Register.

2: For unregistered users, click "Sign Up". On this page, enter the email and username you would like to use and click Ok.

3: You should then receive a validation email with a link. Follow this link to complete the registration process.

4. Once you log in, you will directly jump to the Waiting Room Area. You can click "Join" and join the existing game. You can click "Create A New Game" and create a new game table. And then you will jump to the waiting game area.

5. Once there are six players in the waiting game area, the game will automatically start. If you click "leave", you will return to the waiting room area and join/create a new game.

Additional Information

1. If you are a old user and you join the game. When you log in, you will directly jump to the waiting game area if the game hasn't started, or you will be directed into the game if the game has already started.
